Our Product

Plx SmartHub

A centralized AI-powered home assistant that controls smart lights, thermostats, cameras, and appliances from one sleek interface.

plx Vision AR Glasses

Augmented Reality (AR) glasses that overlay digital content onto the real world — ideal for navigation, fitness, and real-time translations.

Plx Edgebook Pro

A lightweight, high-performance laptop optimized for developers and creators, featuring PLX's own secure OS and cloud sync system.

Plx Airmersh Router

An intelligent Wi-Fi 6 mesh system that self-optimizes for ultra-fast internet across large spaces with built-in cybersecurity features.

Plx VoltPad

A wireless charging pad that powers multiple devices at once (phones, earbuds, smartwatches) using fast and safe power delivery technology.

Plx PluseBand

A sleek wearable that monitors health vitals in real-time using AI analytics — syncing directly with your phone and SmartHub.
